our story

In true COVID era fashion, ours is an arranged marriage-dating over the video call. Our first call was on 4th July 2020.

Video call, chocolate cake & some sky diving

Over a course of the next two months, we both connected over video call, it was Asha's birthday on 14th July when Veeraj sent her a chocolate cake, Asha had the cake and was pleasantly surprised by this gesture. Over the coming days we chatted and shared each other's bucket list, Asha had a tonn, Veeraj only one... Sky Diving! It was 9 am in Sydney and 4.30 am in Bengaluru when Asha pinged Veeraj, to her surprise Veeraj was about to cut off this bucket list item. Veeraj was inching closer towards Asha for this sweet gesture of hers.

We chatted, we talked, ironically our story ran parallel to Netflix's show Indian Matchmaking. While the whole of India ranted about the tradition, here was a couple bonding over the archaic practice.

Adventure begins

It took 10 months before we could see each other in person. By this time the bond, the friendship we shared grew deep. Asha came down to Virar to surprise Veeraj, it was then that we decided on sharing our lives on the eve of Gudipadva. Later Veeraj went to Bengaluru with Asha to meet her parents.

Home Sweet Home

Although we were planning to get married in November this year, God had other plans in mind for us. Thanks to the upcoming third wave, some good timing, and some generous help from our parents, we decided not to waste any more time.

We can't invite all our friends and family due to lockdown restrictions, we still plan to celebrate our day with them by making the event live.
